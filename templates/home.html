Audio Comments

The purpose of this platform is to allow collaboration based on audio tracks. Users can share music, speeches or any other kind of sound and allow others to highlight specific regions with comments.

Explore

Be anonymous if you want

Registration is not required. This means that you can save your tracks and leave comments anonymously. Of couse you can always register and make use of all the features like private recordings, ad-hoc sharing and groups.

In any case please be mindful of others.

Login Register

Upload or Record

Upload audio files or record them directly from the browser. You can listen to what you recorded, assign a name to the track and upload it to the server with few simple clicks. Once uploaded the track will be converted as mp3 to ensure compatibility with most devices.

Regions

Highlight parts of the track, give them a title and leave your comment. The more insightful the better.

Transcriptions

If voice is your sound make use of automated transcriptions. Powered by OpenAI Wispher models you can produce (decent) transcriptions of any spoken word... and if they are not correct you can replace them with yours.
